Track record & method

The headline is a blend of two semi-independent reads on the same quarter: a labour read (hours worked × trend productivity — available early) and an expenditure read (bridge equations mapping ABS partials onto each GDP(E) component — converges to the truth as partials arrive). The blend weight shifts from the labour read to the expenditure read across the release cycle, as estimated by backtest. Shaded bands are 80% intervals from pseudo-real-time backtest errors.
